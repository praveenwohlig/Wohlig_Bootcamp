# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Tone
- Premium
- Aspirational
- Modern
- Minimal

## Allowed Background Colors
- Black
- White
- Warm beige
- Soft gray

## Prohibited Elements
- Weapons
- Alcohol
- Cigarettes
- Political symbols
- Crowded messy environments

## Product Presentation Rules
- Product must remain clearly visible
- No excessive blur
- Luxury commercial aesthetic
- High lighting consistency

## Photography Style
- Cinematic lighting
- Clean composition
- Soft shadows
- Shallow depth of field
