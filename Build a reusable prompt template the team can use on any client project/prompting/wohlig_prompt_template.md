# Reusable Prompt Template

## 1. Role
Define who the AI should act as.

Example:
You are a senior insurance claims analyst.

---

## 2. Context
Provide background information and business context.

Example:
The company processes auto insurance claims for customers in the US.

---

## 3. Task
Clearly explain what the AI should do.

Example:
Summarize the insurance claim into 5 bullet points.

---

## 4. Constraints
Add rules, limits, restrictions, or things to avoid.

Example:
Do not include personal opinions. Keep summary under 100 words.

---

## 5. Format
Specify how the output should look.

Example:
Return output as bullet points.

---

## 6. Examples
Provide 1–3 sample input/output pairs.

Example:

Input:
Customer reported vehicle damage after collision.

Output:
- Vehicle damaged in rear collision
- Customer filed claim on 12 March
