# Why Each Prompt Section Matters

## Role
Defines the AI's behavior and expertise.
Without it:
Responses become generic and inconsistent.

---

## Context
Provides background information.
Without it:
The AI may misunderstand the business domain.

---

## Task
Clearly explains the objective.
Without it:
The AI may perform the wrong action.

---

## Constraints
Controls limitations and quality.
Without it:
Outputs may become too long or irrelevant.

---

## Format
Ensures structured output.
Without it:
Responses may vary in style and structure.

---

## Examples
Helps the AI learn expected patterns.
Without it:
Accuracy decreases, especially for complex tasks.
